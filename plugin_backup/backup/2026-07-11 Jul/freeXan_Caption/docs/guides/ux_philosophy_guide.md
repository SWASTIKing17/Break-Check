# SubMachine UX Philosophy: The Editor's Cockpit

SubMachine is designed to feel like a high-performance cockpit for video editors. Our goal is to minimize friction, maximize information density, and provide tactile feedback for every surgical operation.

## Core Principles

### 1. Slate & Precision
We use a "Slate" (dark, high-contrast) aesthetic to reduce eye strain during long editing sessions. "Precision" comes from our layout, which prioritizes the word-level control of your subtitles.

### 2. Rhythmic Visualization
The **Mini-Timeline** provides a visual "heartbeat" of your sequence. It allows you to see the timing and rhythm of your phrases before you even read the text.

### 3. Tactile Surgery
Every split, merge, and transfer is animated. This isn't just for flair—it provides visual confirmation of where clips are moving, preventing mental disorientation during complex edits.

### 4. Smart Inspector
The Inspector adapts to you. Use **Search** to find hidden properties and **Pinning** to keep your favorite controls (like 'Text Scale' or 'Highlight Color') always at the top.

## New Workflow Features

### Search & Pinning
- **Search**: Tap the search bar in the Inspector to filter properties.
- **Pin**: Click the 📌 icon next to any property name. It will now live in the "Pinned Properties" section at the top of the Inspector.

### The Dashboard
When you first open the Command Center, you're greeted with the **Cockpit Dashboard**. It’s your one-click entry point to scanning your timeline and starting your work.
