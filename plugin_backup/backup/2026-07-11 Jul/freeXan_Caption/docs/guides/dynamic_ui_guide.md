# Guide: The SubMachine Command Center

## What is the Command Center?
The **Command Center** is a new, powerful tab in SubMachine that replaces the need to ever open Premiere Pro's "Essential Graphics Panel." It gives you a birds-eye view of your entire timeline and lets you edit everything in real-time.

## The Two-Column Layout

### 1. The Navigator (Your Map)
On the left side, you'll see your entire video's subtitles arranged in a column.
- **Phrase Cards**: Each group of words is a card.
- **Word Bubbles**: Every single word is a little bubble (like a pill).
- **Click to Snap**: If you click a word bubble, your video playhead instantly jumps to that word. No more hunting for clips on the timeline!

### 2. The Inspector (Your Remote Control)
On the right side, you'll see the settings for the word you just clicked.
- **Real-time Editing**: Change a color or font size, and it updates instantly.
- **Selective Syncing**: Want to change the color of every word in the video? Just toggle the "Sync" switch on the color setting.
- **Locking**: If you have a specific phrase that looks perfect, click the **Lock** icon. Now, even if you sync a new style to the rest of the video, your locked phrase stays exactly how you want it.

## Why this is a Game Changer
- **No More Guesswork**: You can see every word in your video in one list.
- **Batch Editing**: Select multiple bubbles and change them all at once.
- **Integrated Tools**: You can split, join, or add words directly from this menu without leaving your editing flow.

## The Metaphor: The Control Tower
Imagine you are in an **Airport Control Tower**. 
- The **Navigator** is your radar screen—it shows you every plane (word) in the sky.
- The **Inspector** is your radio—it lets you give instructions to one plane or the entire fleet at once.
- The **Lock** is your "Priority Flight" status—it ensures that important planes follow their own special path regardless of what the rest of the fleet is doing.
